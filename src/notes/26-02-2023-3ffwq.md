♫ I recently bought a bunch of CD's on sale. Three albums by **Dark Tranquillity** that I missed from my collection and come recommended on Rate Your Music. *Character* is one of the albums and it's indeed a perfect mix of melancholic and melodic death-metal the band has been known for. The songwriting is a highlight, with almost no fillers and crystal clear production. I do miss Mikael's clean vocals this time around. Check it out on [Songwhip](https://songwhip.com/dark-tranquillity/character). #NowPlaying #Metal #DarkTranquillity
